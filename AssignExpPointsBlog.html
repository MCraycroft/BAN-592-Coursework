Lets start by answering the questions what are expected points and expected points added in football, and what is the significance?. Expected points (ep) are the expected number of points to be scored next. These values can be 8, 7, 6, 3, 2, or 0 and can be represented both positive or negative. If represented negative, these are the points that can be added by the opponent if the ball is turned over to the opponent during the play.
